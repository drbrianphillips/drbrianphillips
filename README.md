## About

Phillips Research & Consulting specializes in advanced hydrologic simulation and 
computational analysis, field evaluation and data collection, and spatial and 
statistical analysis. We serve clients across engineering, research, environmental, 
agricultural, and legal industries, combining industry-standard tools with 
custom-developed software to meet project-specific needs.


## Expertise

**Hydrologic Modeling**  
Application of process-based computational models including DRAINMOD, APEX, and SWAT 
to simulate natural ecosystems, assess current conditions, and evaluate management 
alternatives for drainage design, crop productivity, trafficability, and wetland 
restoration.

**Field Work**  
Field-based data collection integrating ground measurements, drone-based aerial 
surveys, remote sensing, and monitoring station datasets. Dr. Phillips holds FAA 
certification as a Small Unmanned Aircraft Systems (sUAS) Remote Pilot and conducts 
all commercial sUAS operations in accordance with FAA regulations.

**Spatial & Data Analysis**  
GIS, Python, R, and custom computational tools applied to large-scale hydrologic, 
soil, crop, and geospatial datasets. All analytical procedures are designed for 
transparency, reproducibility, and quantitative precision across modeling, spatial 
assessments, and statistical evaluations. Advanced techniques including machine 
learning and AI are applied where warranted.

**Custom Software Development**  
Development of customized computational tools and software enhancements for 
project-specific analytical requirements — including sensitivity and calibration 
analyses, batch simulation workflows, and automated statistical evaluation. All 
tools are designed with reproducibility, documentation, and long-term 
maintainability in mind.

**Expert Witness**  
Technical analysis and testimony in support of federal litigation involving 
hydrology, drainage, and wetland determinations.
